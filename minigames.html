Catch them all

This game is all about catching the delicious fruits!

However the player has to watch out for the dangerous bombs that also drop from the sky. In this chaotic moment players will be able to jump over each other to JOINK the food from each other.

Rotating and lifting the phone will enable the player to move and jump while playing. The person who is able to collect the most fruits in time will win this mini game!

Wind whispers

Flying high in the sky, that is where the second game takes place.

In this game the players have to collect as many hoops as possible. They do however have to watch out for the incoming birds. The player can fly by using any voice input. By either blowing or talking into the mic the player is able to go up or down while playing.

The players do have to watch out that they aren't too quiet because if they don't talk at all they will be eliminated from this game.

Factory Smash

Loud noises and stomping hammers, those are the sounds you hear in mini game three.

This mini game is all about timing. The player have to use their phone as a hammer tool. By lifting their phone upwards and then SMAKING it down the hammers in the game will move.

The player who is able to smash the most scrap pieces will win the game. During the game the factory boss watches over the player and makes sure they work hard!
